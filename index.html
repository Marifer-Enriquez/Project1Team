What's for dinner?

It always happened to us, that we wanted to make something to eat but we didn’t know what to cook, we just stared at the fridge looking at the ingredients but with no idea how to mix and cook them. That’s why we created What for dinner? So we can have an immediate solution.

About
  • About the App
    Have you ever been hungry, looked at the fridge and saw the ingredients but you don’t know what to cook? Well we have the solution for you. With What’s for dinner? You just add the ingredients you want and we’ll look for the perfect recipe for you to cook!
